Spaggetti-Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Spaggetti
    About 500g of spaggetti.
  • Minced Meat
    About 500g of minced pork meat (You can go 50/50 pork/cow meat).
  • Onions
    A big onion or 2 smaller ones will work.
  • Oil
    A tiny bit in a pan so the onion doesn't stick to it.
  • Salt
    Salt.
  • Tomato sauce
    If you can find a brand that has carrots and onion in the sauce that would be great.
  • Rosemary
    This goes in with the onion/s right at the start.
  • Italian Herbs
    I have like 3 different types that I just trow in and it tastes amazing.
  • Tumeric
    Helps the tastes tie together.
  • Soy Sauce
    This is used instead of salt and to give that umami flavor
  • Haragos PistaMinced hot paprika paste, the less spicy variant is Erdos Pista.
    (There is also the sweet version but that is for weak people)

Preparation

  1. Fill a big pot with water and boil, then trow the spaggetti in with a spoon of salt.
  2. Put the oil, minced onion and rosemary into a saucepan.
  3. Simmer until the onion is small
  4. Trow the minced meat in and simmer until the color is brownish (about 15-20 minutes).
  5. Trow in all the spices (no measurements, by hearth as GOD intended).
  6. Simmer a bit more then trow the tomato sauce in.
  7. Add a bit of Soy Sauce (a bit = to your taste)
  8. Leave it to boil/simmer on medium-low heath for 20 minutes while mixing so of then as to not stick to the pan.
  9. After it is done, leave to cool, or splash it on the spaggetti and trow in some parmizian.
